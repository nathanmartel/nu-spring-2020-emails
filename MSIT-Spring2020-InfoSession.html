The Master of Science in Information Technology (MSIT) program in Northwestern University’s McCormick School of Engineering and Applied Science is designed for IT professionals with experience in the workplace. This program’s unique blend of 70 percent technical and 30 percent business and management courses prepares its graduates to strategically plan, implement and manage information technology systems in a broad range of industries.
