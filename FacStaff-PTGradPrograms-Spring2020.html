Choose from our graduate-level, part-time programs to advance your career

Northwestern Engineering offers five higher education programs geared toward working professionals that provide the flexibility to maintain an active work and life schedule while pursuing a master’s degree. Each program is offered part-time and has ample administrative support to help you focus your energy on study.

Our programs design their curricula to build on the skills you’ve already developed in the workplace, allowing for professional growth that can be immediately applied to your job. Take advantage of degrees that advance your technical knowledge and enhance business acumen, helping you become a more complete, valued, and effective leader.

Master of Engineering Management

The MEM program is designed for engineering and science professionals who want to develop core management and leadership skills while staying on the cutting edge of technology.

Master of Science in Information Technology

The MSIT program is for information technology professionals who want to improve and broaden their technical skills while learning business principles.

Master of Product Design and Development Management

The MPD2 program is highly focused on students learning product design and development and the management of that process.

Master of Science in Executive Management for Design and Construction

The EMDC online program equips current and future executives with the enhanced skills needed to become successful, senior-level leaders in the design and construction industries.

Master of Project Management

The MPM program is designed to prepare you for leadership roles in the construction, operation, and management of major civil and environmental engineering projects.
